# YouTube Tech-Business Channel Plan

This document outlines the strategy, monetization projections, 24-week content calendar, and script templates for the YouTube channel focusing on AI, Web Development, App Development, and Business.

## Channel Strategy & Monetization Profile
- **Niche**: Reverse-engineering successful business models, building their core MVP feature, and explaining the technical architecture.
- **Target Audience**: Entrepreneurs, developers, freelancers, and tech enthusiasts.
- **Primary Language**: Hinglish (Conversational Hindi with English technical terms) for maximum reach and high local retention in India.
- **Estimated Monetization Thresholds**: 
  - Fan Funding: 500 Subscribers / 3,000 Watch Hours.
  - AdSense: 1,000 Subscribers / 4,000 Watch Hours (expected in 6-9 months).
- **RPM (India Tech/Business Niche)**: ₹80 to ₹180 per 1,000 views.

### Projected Monthly Income (at 6 Months / 24 Weeks)
- **YouTube AdSense**: ₹15,000 - ₹35,000 (based on 1.5L - 3L views/month).
- **Brand Sponsorships**: ₹30,000 - ₹70,000 (2 integrations/month).
- **Affiliate & Digital Sales (GitHub Gated Code)**: ₹10,000 - ₹25,000.
- **Total Estimated Income**: **₹55,000 - ₹1,30,000 per month**.
