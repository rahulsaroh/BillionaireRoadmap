**Dialogue (Hinglish):**

```
If (Time_Difference < 24 Hours) -> Keep Streak Active (Double complete safe zone)
If (24 Hours <= Time_Difference < 48 Hours) -> Increment Streak Fire!
If (Time_Difference >= 48 Hours) -> Danger Zone! Trigger Reset Loop.

```
